Updated Customer Financing Flow

Key Changes to the Application Flow

1. The Terms Step now shows FCRA disclosure with consent checkboxes instead of upfront financing terms.
2. A new Financing Offer Step has been added after bank connection to show qualified financing terms.
3. The overall number of steps remains the same, but with improved disclosure compliance.

Step 1: Pre-Qualification
FCRA Disclosure & Consent
• FCRA disclaimer for credit check
• Consent checkbox for credit pull
• Privacy policy acceptance
• No financing details shown yet
→
Step 2: Identity Verification
Complete KYC process
• Verify customer identity
• DiDit verification service
• Protect against fraud
• Regulatory compliance
→
Step 3: Bank Connection
Link bank via Plaid
• Connect checking account
• Verify account ownership
• Set up for ACH payments
• Financial data collected
→
Step 4: Financing Offer
Show qualified terms
• Display ShiFi financing terms
• Based on credit qualification
• Show APR, term, and payments
• Customer accepts offer
→
Step 5: Payment Schedule
Confirm payment details
• Set payment due dates
• Confirm monthly amount
• Review total cost
• Schedule first payment
→
Step 6: Contract Signing
Sign financing agreement
• Review final agreement
• E-signature process
• Get contract copy
• Complete the process
